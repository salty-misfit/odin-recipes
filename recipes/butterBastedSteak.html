Butter Basted Steak

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemaryt

Steps

  1. Get a heavy bottom pan REALLY hot
  2. Put steak on pan leave alone for 2-3 minutes
  3. if nice crust has formed flip steak and leave it for half the time.
  4. add butter garlic and rosemary then baste until steak reaches doneness.
